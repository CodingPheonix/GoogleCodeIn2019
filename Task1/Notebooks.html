Choosing a notebook

My notebook:

In my experience, any paper will do, as it does not make much difference when you are at a developing stage in your art. At a professional level of course some artists have there perferences in terms paper i.e. thickness, texture, and color. I encourage begininners to simply doodle on anything and everything, from newpapers to school notebooks or even a toiletpaper!

Name Description Cost in USD
Stillman & Birn Zeta Sketchbooks (recommended) Has nice texture too it with heavy pages with a smooth finish that some artists will really like. It also is universal in that it works well with a number of possible mediums allowing for artistic expression. Great value! $10-$20
Rhodia Webnotebooks Nice, smooth ivory paper, takes well to ink easily as well as coming with a lattice grid which allows for some more complex construction. Not to mention it is just a good all round notebook to have. $20 - $30
