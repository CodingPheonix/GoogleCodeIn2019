Description:

Ever since I could hold a pencil, I have always been fasinated with colors, drawing, and sketching. My imagination was rabid, everywhere I went I saw faces and people. Clouds became paintings, trees became creatures moving silently in the wind, unnoticed, rocks a masterpiece of the earth. Nature around me became the canvas for my mind to feast on. I soon learned to doodle everything that I saw down, in my books. Everywhere I went I doodled, in classrooms, at home, even on my exam papers! Soon it became as natural as breathing, and my prefered way of expression.

Pros

  • Fun to get started
  • Very intuitive
  • Lot's of tutorials and guides
  • Easy to learn
  • Meditative/theraputic

Cons

  • Hard to maintain skill (like practicing music)
  • Requires passion, focus and attention
  • Like poetry, is daunting to begin
  • Don't expect to be amazing at first
